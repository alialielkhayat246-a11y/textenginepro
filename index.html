📘 How to Use Every Tool

🔍 Detect Language

What it does: Identifies the actual language of your text using the franc trigram engine covering 175+ languages. Type or paste text, then click Detect Language. Works best with 20+ characters.

🧠 Smart Fix

What it does: Fixes text typed with the wrong keyboard layout. If you typed Arabic words while in English mode (or vice versa), Smart Fix detects the dominant language and converts automatically.

Example: lvpf (wrong layout) ➡ بلاي

EN ➡ AR  /  AR ➡ EN

Manually converts between Arabic and English keyboard layouts key-by-key. Use when you know which direction you mis-typed.

🔤 Capitalize

Converts to Title Case. the quick brown fox ➡ The Quick Brown Fox

📢 UPPERCASE  /  📝 lowercase

Instantly switch the case of all letters in your text.

🧹 Clean Space

Removes double spaces, extra tabs, and leading/trailing whitespace.

✅ Copy  /  🗑 Clear

Copy sends the full text to your clipboard. Clear wipes the editor and the auto-saved draft.

🚀 Tech Behind the Tool

Text Engine Pro is 100% client-side. Your text never leaves your browser. We use localStorage to auto-save your draft — no server, no accounts, no tracking.

EN ⇄ AR Keyboard Mapping

A precise key-position hash map of the standard Arabic QWERTY layout. The two-character ligature لا (B key) is handled via priority lookahead, running in O(n) time.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text data private?

Yes. Fully serverless — your text is processed only in your browser and never transmitted anywhere.

Why is Smart Fix converting the wrong way?

Smart Fix counts Arabic vs Latin characters. If your text is mixed, add more characters of the correct language or use the manual EN➡AR / AR➡EN buttons instead.

Can I use this for PDF text?

Yes. Paste PDF text here, fix the layout or encoding, then paste back into your document.

Does this site use cookies?

The tool itself uses localStorage only. Google AdSense ads may use cookies. See our Privacy Policy.
